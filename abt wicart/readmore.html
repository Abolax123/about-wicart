THE OVERVIEW

WiCart is a new solution to the problem in e-commerce built by team of young and vibrant developers, intelligent and creative minds. WiCart hope to cover many loopholes and proffer solutions to the issues in the area of e-commerce and its effectiveness. WiCart is an international and global minded digital startup whose ultimate goal is to provide an easy and user-friendly platform as we understand that the future is digital. WiCart is set to solve the issues of indirect interaction of consumers and their desired product among most SMEs without any need for physical interaction. We also aim to promote the products of SMEs that are registered on the platform and let them reach more audiences and grow their numbers. WiCart is also dedicated to providing advanced bespoke solution to product authenticity and easy mode of payment as we are very concerned about the quality of product and goods that will be traded on the platform. Having your products on the platform will bring about exposure and growth in numbers of your customers around the world. Integration of online payment, product authentication, instant delivery across the country and to anywhere around the globe.

Why use WiCart?
For Business owners

-WiCart brings a very big opportunity to SMEs to have their products hosted on a website without having to spam customers while trying to check out their products.
- It allows for Vendors or business owners to have the full authority on their products even after its been hosted on the website
- WiCart runs free advertisement and allows registered vendors on the website reach more audience and increase their customers
- WiCart also offers a program which serves as source of investment for businesses hosted on the platform and brings percentage of return on sales made by each business owner.

Why use WiCart?
For consumers

- Users/ Final consumers have variety of opportunities while trading with WiCart one of which is the full interaction with different products on their vendor’s store hosted on the platform
- Convenient mode of payment (online payment integration), 24/7 trading available.
- Vouchers are available after each purchase which can be accumulated to purchase another product on the website
- Instant delivery of products of authenticated products
- 24hrs within state return of product and 48hrs outside state
